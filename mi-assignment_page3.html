How to Get from LAX to Koreatown

By Taxi

  1. Catch a taxi directly from LAX to Koreatown.
  2. It’s a convenient and quick option.
  3. Takes approximately 30-40 minutes depending on traffic.

By Bus

  1. Take the FlyAway Bus from LAX to Union Station.
  2. Transfer to the Metro Red or Purple Line to get to Koreatown.
  3. This budget-friendly option takes around 1-1.5 hours.

By Rideshare

  1. Use rideshare services like Uber or Lyft.
  2. Request a ride from LAX to Koreatown.
  3. Travel time is similar to taking a taxi.

By Metro

  1. Take the Green Line from Aviation/LAX Station.
  2. Transfer to the Blue Line at Willowbrook/Rosa Parks Station.
  3. Then transfer to the Red or Purple Line to reach Koreatown.
  4. It’s an economical option but takes longer, around 1.5-2 hours.
