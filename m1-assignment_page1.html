Welcome to Koreatown Community Board

Discover the vibrant and diverse neighborhood of Koreatown in Los Angeles. From delicious food and exciting nightlife to unique shopping experiences and cultural landmarks, Koreatown has something for everyone.

Things to Do

Explore a variety of activities and attractions in Koreatown. Whether you're interested in historical sites, modern entertainment, or family-friendly activities, there's always something to do.

  • Visit Historical Sites: Explore the rich history of Koreatown by visiting landmarks like the Wiltern Theatre and the Chapman Plaza.
  • Experience K-pop Culture: Dive into the world of K-pop with visits to K-pop stores and themed cafes.
  • Relax in Korean Spas: Unwind at one of the many Korean spas offering traditional treatments and modern amenities.

Eat & Drink

Koreatown is famous for its diverse culinary scene, offering everything from traditional Korean BBQ to trendy dessert shops.

Korean BBQ

The sizzle and smoke of tabletop Korean barbecue are a must-try. Visit popular spots like Chosun Galbee and Soot Bull Jeep for an unforgettable dining experience.

Coffee & Desserts

Enjoy a relaxing afternoon at one of Koreatown's many coffee houses and dessert cafes. Try specialties like patbingsoo (shaved ice) and artisanal cakes.

Find Events

  • Night Markets: Experience the vibrant night markets with food stalls, live music, and unique crafts.
  • Cultural Festivals: Celebrate Korean culture with events like the annual Korean Festival featuring performances, food, and activities.
  • Live Music & Entertainment: Enjoy live performances at local venues and bars.
